FORMULA 1

The pinnacle of motorsport, Formula 1 represents the ultimate in racing technology and driver skill. With speeds exceeding 370 km/h and G-forces that push drivers to their limits, F1 is the most prestigious racing championship in the world.

Extreme Performance

0-100 km/h in 2.6 seconds

Hybrid Power

1.6L V6 turbo hybrid engines

Global Reach

23 races across 5 continents

F1
F2

FORMULA 2

The final stepping stone to Formula 1, Formula 2 showcases the world's most promising young racing talent. Using identical cars to ensure the focus is on driver ability, F2 provides thrilling wheel-to-wheel action at circuits around the world.

Proving Ground

Where future F1 stars are born

Powerful Machines

620hp 3.4L V6 turbo engines

Championship Path

Super License points awarded

FORMULA 3

The first major step on the road to Formula 1, Formula 3 is where young drivers learn their craft against equally talented rivals. With less downforce than F2 but similar power-to-weight ratios, F3 cars demand precision and commitment from their drivers.

Youth Development

Average age of just 18 years

Balanced Performance

380hp 3.4L naturally aspirated

Shared Weekends

Runs alongside F1 and F2 events

F3
FE

FORMULA E

The electric future of motorsport is here. Formula E combines cutting-edge electric vehicle technology with thrilling street circuit racing in the heart of the world's greatest cities. With instant torque and regenerative braking, FE cars deliver a unique and sustainable racing spectacle.

Sustainable Racing

Zero emissions, 100% electric

Urban Showdowns

Street circuits in major cities

Fast Charging

350kW charging capability
